Privacy Policy for Zonarc

Last updated: 20 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the developer of Zonarc ("we", "us") handles your information in the Zonarc — Time Zone Converter mobile application (the "App"). By using the App you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

We do not collect any personal information. The App has no account, no analytics, no advertising, and no servers of our own. Your saved clocks, groups, preferences, and purchase status are stored only on your device and never leave it. We never sell or share your data, because we never receive it.

Purchases

The optional one-time "unlimited clocks" purchase is processed entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment details. Google's handling of payment information is governed by Google's own privacy policy.

Home-screen widget

The optional home-screen widget reads your saved clocks from on-device storage only; nothing is sent off your device.

Permissions

The App requests no special device permissions. Your clocks, groups and preferences stay on your device at all times.

Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data retention and deletion

Your clocks, groups, preferences, and purchase status are stored only on your device; uninstalling the App removes them. There is no other data to retain or delete, since the App collects none.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date above.
